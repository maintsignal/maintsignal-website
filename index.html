Methodology

No black boxes. Here's how we calculate everything.

Transparent enough for your reliability engineer to defend to leadership.

Data Quality

Weighted scoring across three dimensions

40% completeness (critical fields filled), 30% consistency (date logic, duplicates, format), 30% usability (generic codes like "Other" flagged). Every field scored individually.

AI Normalization

Abbreviation dictionary + LLM classification

200+ term expansion layer, then industry-specific LLM prompt (ISO 14224, GMP, FSMA context), then confidence scoring. Low-confidence results flagged, not guessed. Raw mapping provided for spot-checking.

Downtime

Calculated from timestamps, estimated when missing

Malfunction end minus start = downtime. When timestamps are missing, estimated from created-to-completed time. Every estimate labeled. MTTR and MTBF calculated per asset.

Financial Impact

Your production value times our downtime hours

You provide production $/hour (or we use industry defaults). Loss = downtime x rate. Every assumption stated in the report. Purpose: frame failures in business terms.

Compliance

Audit gap analysis against 5 frameworks

FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ISO 55001, IATF 16949, FSMA, OSHA PSM. Each required field checked. Pass/warn/fail per field. Prioritized remediation actions generated.

Root Cause

Failure chain and correlation analysis

Detects when failure A consistently triggers failure B. Identifies chronic repeat failures. Finds co-occurring failures suggesting shared root cause. Calculates cascade rates.

Benchmarking

Compare against industry peer averages

PM ratio, MTTR, failure code usage, cost per work order, reactive %. Rated as world-class, above average, below average, or critical gap. Sources cited.

Questions

Frequently asked.

What file formats do you accept?

CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), PDF, JSON, and XML. Any export from any system works - SAP, Maximo, Oracle, MaintainX, UpKeep, Fiix, Limble, or even manual spreadsheets. We auto-detect and map your columns.

How do I send my data securely?

After signing the NDA, you can email the file directly to us, upload via our secure link, or use SFTP for larger files. We provide the secure transfer method that works best for your IT requirements.

Is the pilot assessment really free? What's the catch?

Yes. Pilot partners receive the full 11-module assessment at zero cost. No credit card, no commitment. We offer this because the report demonstrates its own value - once you see your data quality score and hidden patterns, most teams want monthly monitoring.

What data fields do you need?

At minimum: work order ID, equipment/asset ID, date, and description. Ideal exports also include failure codes, cause codes, timestamps, labor hours, and cost. The more fields, the deeper the analysis - but we work with whatever you have.

Does your AI access our raw data for training?

No. Your data is never used for model training. AI classification happens on isolated, US-hosted infrastructure. Raw data is deleted within 30 days of report delivery. This is in the NDA.

What happens after the pilot assessment?

You decide. If the findings are valuable, we offer monthly intelligence ($1,499/month) for ongoing trend tracking. If not, you keep the report and owe nothing. No pressure, no follow-up calls unless you want them.
